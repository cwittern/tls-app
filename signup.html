Application for membership in the TLS Project

The TLS Project is a collaborative research project, concerned with all aspects of the Chinese culture as transmitted through its textual sources.

This website is the main platform for this work. It allows to prepare, edit, analyze, annotate and translate the texts.

This system is still work in progress, but a limited number of accounts are available at this moment. The accounts are free of charge and there is no advertisement or tracking on the site. Your personal data will only be kept on the server and not shared with anyone. If you request deletion of the account, all personal information will be deleted, except your name as far as it is associated with data items.
